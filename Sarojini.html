About her :

Sarojini Naidu was an Indian independence activist and poetess who earned the sobriquet of Nightingale of India. She was born in a Bengali Hindu family in Hyderabad.
She was educated in Chennai, London and Cambridge. She married Dr. Govindarajulu Naidu and settled down in Hyderabad.

One of her poem : Autumn Song

Like a joy on the heart of a sorrow,
The sunset hangs on a cloud,
A golden storm of glittering sheaves,
Of fair and frail and fluttering leaves,
The wild wind blows in a cloud.

Glossary:

  1. Sheaves : Pulley
  2. Glittering : Shinning
